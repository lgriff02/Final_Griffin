What goes into the seats: A breakdown of what leads to NHL attendance success

In October 2021, the National Hockey League commenced the first (hopefully) fully normal season since 2018-19. The 82-game schedule and the excitement of full capacity crowds are back. In the 2018-19 season, many factors contributed to how often a team's arena was flooded with fans every night. These factors include, but are not limited to, team success, capacity, and the amount of tickets offered by the arena.
